The pyramids were constructed by crews of between 20,000 and 30,000 men and took up to 20 years to build. With the election of a new Pharaoh a new pyramid was constructed. Pyramids were produced so that they may become the Pharaohs' final resting places once the they died. The pyramid had to be completed before the Pharaoh's death else he will not pass to the next life.
